# 🎓 Conference Management Platform - User Story Guide
*(A simple explanation for non-technical users)*

---

## 🌟 Welcome to Our Conference Platform!

Imagine you are visiting a digital hub where academic conferences come to life. This platform connects researchers, speakers, and attendees from around the world. Here's how your journey works:

---

## 🚶 The User Journey: Step by Step

### 1️⃣ **First Visit: Exploring the Conference**
When you first arrive at our website, you don't need an account yet! You can:
- **Browse Active Conferences**: See what events are currently accepting submissions
- **View Themes**: Discover topics like "Artificial Intelligence", "Climate Change", or "Medical Innovations"
- **Meet Speakers**: Read about keynote speakers and their expertise
- **Check Schedule**: See planned sessions and presentation times
- **View Committee**: Meet the organizing team behind the event

> 💡 **Think of this like walking into a conference hall and picking up a brochure before deciding to attend.**

---

### 2️⃣ **Submitting Your Research Paper**
You're a researcher with a brilliant paper to share. Here's what happens:

**Step A: Find an Active Conference**
- You look for a conference that is currently **OPEN** (active)
- If no conference is active, you'll see: *"No active conference found"*
- This means organizers haven't opened submissions yet, or the deadline has passed

**Step B: Submit Your Paper**
- You fill out a submission form with:
  - Your paper title and abstract
  - List of all co-authors (names, emails, affiliations)
  - Choose a theme/topic for your paper
- **Important**: You do NOT create an account yet! Your email is just recorded as an author

**Step C: Track Your Submission**
- You receive a **Submission ID** (like: `SUB-2025-0001`)
- Use this ID later to check if your paper was:
  - ✅ **Accepted** → You can present!
  - ❌ **Rejected** → Try another conference
  - ⏳ **Pending** → Under review by experts

> 💡 **Your paper is like entering a contest. You get a ticket number (Submission ID) to check results later.**

---

### 3️⃣ **Getting Accepted & Registering as a Participant**
Great news! Your paper was accepted (or you just want to attend). Now you become a **Participant**:

**Step A: Create Your Participant Account**
- You register with your **email**, name, affiliation, and country
- This creates your **permanent profile** in our system
- You get a **Participant ID** (like: `PART-2025-0042`)

**Step B: Register for the Specific Conference**
- Your participant account is now linked to this specific conference
- You get a **Registration ID** (like: `REG-2025-0089`)
- This confirms your spot at the event

> 💡 **Think of it like:**
> - **Participant Account** = Your library card (works everywhere)
> - **Registration** = Checking out a specific book for this event only

---

### 4️⃣ **Presenting Your Work (For Speakers)**
If you're giving a presentation:

**Invited Speakers** (Keynote):
- Organizers add you directly to the system
- You're assigned to a **Session** (time slot + room)
- Your bio appears on the conference website

**Paper Presenters** (Authors):
- After acceptance, organizers schedule your session
- You present your research to the audience
- Your name appears in the conference program

---

### 5️⃣ **Receiving Your Certificate**
After the conference ends:

**Automatic Certificate Generation**:
- The system creates a **unique certificate** for you
- Includes: Your name, conference name, date, and a **verification code**
- Types of certificates:
  - 📜 **Participant Certificate** → For attending
  - 🎤 **Speaker Certificate** → For presenting
  - ✍️ **Author Certificate** → For published papers

**How to Get It**:
- Download directly from the website
- Verify authenticity using the unique code
- Share on LinkedIn or include in your CV

> 💡 **Each certificate has a secret code (like a QR code) that proves it's real. Anyone can verify it online!**

---

## 🔍 Why Did You See "No Active Conference Found"?

This message appears when:
1. **Organizers haven't created a conference yet** → The system is empty
2. **Conference is not marked as "Active"** → It's in draft mode or ended
3. **Wrong timing** → Submissions closed or haven't opened yet

**Solution**: Contact the conference organizers to activate the event!

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Can I create an account without a conference?**  
A: No! You only create a participant account when registering for an active conference.

**Q: What if I have multiple papers?**  
A: Submit each paper separately. All will be linked to your email. When you register, all your accepted papers are associated with your participant profile.

**Q: Can I attend without presenting?**  
A: Yes! Register as a participant even if you didn't submit a paper.

**Q: How do I verify a certificate?**  
A: Enter the certificate code on the "Verify Certificate" page. It will show the person's name, conference, and issue date.

**Q: Who can see my email?**  
A: Only conference admins. Other users see your name and affiliation publicly.

## 🛠️ For Administrators (Behind the Scenes)

While users enjoy the platform, admins work in a protected dashboard to:
- ✅ Create and activate conferences
- ✅ Review submitted papers (Accept/Reject)
- ✅ Add speakers and schedule sessions
- ✅ Generate certificates automatically
- ✅ Manage committee members
- ✅ View statistics (submissions, registrations, etc.)

They log in with special credentials that regular users never see.

## 🌐 Summary: How Everything Connects

```
📢 Conference (The Event)
   ├── 📝 Submissions (Papers waiting for review)
   ├── 🎤 Speakers (Invited presenters)
   ├── 🕐 Sessions (Scheduled talks)
   ├── 🏷️ Themes (Topics like AI, Medicine, etc.)
   ├── 👥 Participants (Registered attendees)
   │    └── 📜 Certificates (Proof of attendance/presentation)
   └── 🎖️ Committee (Organizing team)
```

**The Golden Rule**: 
> **No Active Conference = No Registration, No Submissions, No Certificates**
> 
> Everything starts with an active conference!
